My name is Declan Kutscher and I am a Master's student at the University of Pittsburgh. I currently do machine learning research with [Xiaowei Jia](https://sites.pitt.edu/~xiaowei/) where I am focused on solving real-world problems in the domain of climate and ecological systems. I have a great interest in computer vision, remote sensing, and self-supervised learning. My master’s thesis topic is to be decided but, I am currently exploring the use of self-supervised learning (JEPA or other) for ecological system monitoring in satellite imagery(and other modalities).

The works that inspired my aspirations include:

- Yann Lecun's [JEPA](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=BZ5a1r-kVsf) paper
- Mahmoud Assran's [I-JEPA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.08243) paper
- Ritwik Gupta's [xT](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2403.01915) paper
- [x-View3-SAR](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2206.00897) challenge

Feel free to contact me at dtk28 ‘at’ pitt ‘dot’ edu. I am always open to new opportunities for research and collaboration.
